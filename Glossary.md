# SOC Glossary

A quick reference for common SOC and Blue Team terminology.

---

## SOC
Security Operations Center – team responsible for monitoring, detecting, and responding to security incidents.

## Alert
A notification triggered by a detection rule indicating suspicious activity.

## Incident
A confirmed security event requiring investigation and response.

## SIEM
Security Information and Event Management – central platform for log collection and correlation.

## EDR
Endpoint Detection and Response – endpoint-focused visibility and response tool.

## IOC
Indicator of Compromise – artifact suggesting malicious activity.

## TTPs
Tactics, Techniques, and Procedures used by attackers.

## LOLBins
Living-Off-The-Land Binaries – legitimate system tools abused by attackers.

## False Positive
Benign activity incorrectly flagged as malicious.

## Triage
Initial analysis to assess alert severity and scope.

## Lateral Movement
Attacker movement between systems inside a network.
