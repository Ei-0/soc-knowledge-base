# What is a SOC (Security Operations Center)?

A **Security Operations Center (SOC)** is a centralized function responsible for continuously **monitoring, detecting, investigating, and responding** to cybersecurity threats within an organization.

The SOC acts as the **defensive nerve center**, ensuring threats are identified early and handled effectively.
